# FAQ

Quick answers — the canonical list also lives at [FAQ.md](../FAQ.md).

## Is MusicChain i3 a cryptocurrency?

It is a **music platform** with **in-app resources** (McCC, Fuel, Gas, ITC). These are economy mechanics for participation and influence — not a promise of external market value.

## Can I mine McCC?

No proof-of-work mining. McCC is generated through **documented activities** (listening, interactions, chain rules).

## Where can I verify reward math?

- [TOKENOMICS.md](../TOKENOMICS.md)  
- [musicchain-tokenomics/](./musicchain-tokenomics/)  
- MCIPs in [musicchain-specifications/](./musicchain-specifications/)

## Why is the full backend not open source?

To protect users: secrets, admin tools, and abuse prevention stay **private** until they can be published safely. Core **rules** and **rates** are public here.

## What is a listen triad?

One playtime commit that atomically attributes rewards to **listener**, **creator**, and **MusicChain** with a shared event id. See [economy.md](./economy.md).

## What are PIA and TIA?

- **PIA** — Profile Interaction Analytics  
- **TIA** — Track Interaction Analytics (likes, boosts, comments on tracks)

## What is ITC for?

**Influence Token Credit** — long-horizon impact, epochs, matrix, network rewards. Different from per-second McCC.

## Is the mobile app available?

**Android** — beta / early access via [musicchaini3.com/app](https://musicchaini3.com/app).  
**iPhone** — PWA or internal Expo builds; App Store listing planned.

## How do I contribute?

See [CONTRIBUTING.md](../CONTRIBUTING.md). Documentation and MCIPs are the best starting points in Phase 1.

## Is this financial advice?

No. Documentation describes system behavior only.

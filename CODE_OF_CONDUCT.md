# Code of Conduct

We adopt the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in MusicChain i3 a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to project maintainers via the security/contact channel listed in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md). All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.

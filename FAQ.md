# FAQ

## Is MusicChain i3 a cryptocurrency?

It is a **music platform** with **in-app resources** (McCC, Fuel, Gas, ITC). These are economy mechanics for participation and influence — not a promise of external market value.

## Can I mine McCC?

No proof-of-work mining. McCC is generated through **documented activities** (listening, interactions, chain rules).

## Where can I verify reward math?

- [TOKENOMICS.md](./TOKENOMICS.md)
- [../musicchain-tokenomics/](../musicchain-tokenomics/)

## Why is the full backend not open source?

To protect users: secrets, admin tools, and abuse prevention are **private** until we can publish them safely. Core **rules** and **rates** are public.

## What is a listen triad?

One playtime commit that atomically attributes rewards to **listener**, **creator**, and **MusicChain** with a shared event id. See [docs/Economy.md](./docs/Economy.md).

## What are PIA and TIA?

- **PIA** — Profile Interaction Analytics (actions on profiles)
- **TIA** — Track Interaction Analytics (likes, boosts, comments, etc. on tracks)

## What is ITC for?

**Influence Token Credit** — long-horizon impact, epochs, matrix, network rewards. Different from per-second McCC.

## What is the 313 Matrix?

Community ranking / field system (see site Matrix pages). Formulas are being documented via MCIPs.

## How do I propose a rule change?

Draft an MCIP. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md).

## GitHub or GitLab?

We plan **GitHub** for maximum visibility for Phase 1. GitLab may be used for private CI later.

## Is this financial advice?

No. Documentation describes system behavior only.
